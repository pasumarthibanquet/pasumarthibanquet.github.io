About Us

Located in the heart of the city, Pasumarthi Banquet Hall is a modern venue perfect for birthdays, corporate events, and social gatherings. The AC banquet hall can accommodate up to 500 guests, making it an ideal venue for a variety of events. It is equipped with a full AV setup and offers catering services to suit your needs. The hall can be beautifully decorated for all kinds of functions, ensuring a memorable experience. Ample parking space is available, including a double cellar, and two lifts operate 24 hours for convenient access.
