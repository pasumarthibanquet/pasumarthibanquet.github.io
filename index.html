About Us

Creating memories that last a lifetime

Located in the heart of Khammam, Pasumarthi Banquet Hall is a premier venue perfect for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and social gatherings. Our AC banquet hall can accommodate up to 500 guests, making it an ideal venue for a variety of celebrations.


We are equipped with a full AV setup and offer premium catering services to suit your needs. Our hall can be beautifully decorated for all kinds of functions, ensuring a memorable experience. Ample parking space is available, including a double cellar, and two lifts operate 24 hours for convenient access.
